Ingredients:
* Can kidney beans
* Can golden wax beans
* Can cannellini beans
* ½ red onion
* 2 stalks celery
* ¼ cup vegetable oil
* ⅓ cup cider vinegar
* ¼ cup sugar
* salt
* pepper
* 1 tbsp rosemary

Directions:
1. Drain the beans
2. Put all ingredients in a bowl except oil and vinegar
3. Mix the oil and vinegar together and pour over ingredients
